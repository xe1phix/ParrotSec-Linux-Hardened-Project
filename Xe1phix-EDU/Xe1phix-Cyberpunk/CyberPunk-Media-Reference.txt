DISCLAIMER
	The following lists should be considered an incomplete work-in-progess.
	Other than the ESSENTIALS, everything listed is not a de facto cyberpunk masterpiece.
	Opinions on the following material can and will vary depending upon personal taste.
	Proto-cyberpunk and post-cyberpunk material are not categorized separately.
	If you find anything missing, inappropriate, or completely wrong, please yell.
