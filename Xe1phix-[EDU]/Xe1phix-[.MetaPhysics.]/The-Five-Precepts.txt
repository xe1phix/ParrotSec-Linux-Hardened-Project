☸️☸️ The Five Precepts ☸️☸️

1) I undertake the precept to refrain from killing or harming living beings, on purpose.

2) I undertake the precept to refrain from taking what is not given.

3) I undertake the precept to refrain from any wrong sexual activity.

4) I undertake to keep the precept to abstain from telling lies, harsh speech, gossip, or slander.

5) I undertake the precept to refrain from intoxicating drinks and drugs which lead to carelessness.
