    [?] This script has multiple points of execution
        It executes syntax from the following service managers:
## --------------------------------------------------------------- ## 
    [+] service
    [+] update-rc.d, 
    [+] chkconfig
    [+] systemctl
    [+] /etc/init.d/*



##-===========================================================================================-##
##                      [+] DisableServicesParrotKios - Batch Actions:
##-===========================================================================================-##
## ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- ## 
     [+] Stop - the selected service (Using each of the service maintainence programs)
## ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- ## 
     [+] Disable - Stop the process from starting in the future
## ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- ##

## ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- ## 
    [?] You can uncomment lines at the bottom to be a more forceful admin.

##-===========================================================================================-##


##-===========================================================================================-##
##                      [+] Disabled Actions (Uncomment it out to enable) 
##-===========================================================================================-##
## ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- ## 
    [+] Mask - Replace the service config with /dev/null (Disabled By Default) (systemctl)
## ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- ## 
    [+] Remove - Remove all links for a script (update-rc.d)
